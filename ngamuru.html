Ngamuru Advisory

Ngamuru Advisory is a joint venture of Clayton Utz, set up to offer strategic advisory and commercial services to the Commonwealth Government and affiliated agencies. It's purpose is to offer early advice in projects, ensuring their success.

The firm is built on a wealth of experience and understanding of the advisory industry, hence the brand idea of 'Greater experience, stronger outcomes'.

The concept shown here draws on the idea of knowledge and the non-linear nature of projects. It visualises the strength and sophistication of the firm and tells the story of their years of experience.

Designed at Folk in collaboration with Creative Director Graham Barton and Design Director Gary Walmsley.
