Clare Barrett

Designer and illustrator

Chiropractors' Association Australia

Every year CAA roll out a campaign to raise awareness with the public about Chiropractic. This extends to the members of CAA, who receive the campaign collateral to advertise from their own Chiropractic business.

This concept was designed to have a lead poster and follow up posters. The lead, leering and ominous, designed to evoke the debilitating feeling of back pain. As the message 'Silence back pain' is read, the pain disappears. The follow up posters do the reverse. Words to describe everyday activities impacted by back pain are restored, with the tagline 'Get Back to (everyday activity)'.

Designed at Folk in collaboration with Creative Director Graham Barton.
