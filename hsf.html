Herbert Smith Freehills

Herbert Smith Freehills is a global professional services business, with a focus on legal services. They're experts across a number of sectors, real estate being one of many.

For this project, the aim was to create an infographic that visually summed up the top five real estate trends for 2016, and to produce illustrations to pinpoint each of the five. The result is a sleek, cohesive design in keeping with the HSF brand.

Designed at Folk.
