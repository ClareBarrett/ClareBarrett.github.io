Clare Barrett

Designer and illustrator based in Sydney, Australia.

BoardLinks

BoardLinks is an initiative connecting a database of leading women to be considered for Australian Government board appointments.

Through the aid of this initiative the Australian Government is aiming to achieve its gender diversity target of a 50/50 ratio of men and women on boards.

This lead the brand idea for BoardLinks, equality in the form of a 50/50 ratio. This influenced the logo, and became a unique graphic device that talks to the heart of the cause.

Designed at Folk in collaboration with Creative Director Graham Barton and Design Director James Welch.
